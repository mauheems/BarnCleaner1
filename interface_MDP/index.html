      Getting Started:

      Welcome to the Mapping Tab! Here, you can initiate the mapping process for your robot to create a map of its environment.

      Instructions for Mapping:

      To start mapping, click on the "Start Mapping" button below. Once mapping is started, the robot will begin exploring its environment to create a map. You can monitor the mapping progress in the status log.

      Mapping Process:

      During mapping, the robot will move around, scanning and recording the features of its surroundings. It will create a map based on the collected data, which will be used for navigation of the robots.

      Ending Mapping:

      To stop the mapping process, click on the "Stop Mapping" button below. Once mapping is stopped, the generated map will be available for use.

      Note:

      Ensure that the robot has enough battery power to complete the mapping process. Avoid interrupting the mapping process once started to ensure accurate mapping results.

      Additional Assistance:

      If you encounter any issues or need further assistance, feel free to contact our support team for help.
